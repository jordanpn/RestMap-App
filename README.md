## About

RestMap uses AI-powered quality predictions to help you find clean, well-maintained restrooms before you arrive. Perfect for road trips, families with young children, rideshare drivers, and anyone who values knowing what to expect.

### Key Features

- **RestRoom IQ** — AI analyzes 250+ chains and community data to predict quality with letter grades (A-F)
- **Road Trip Mode** — Track your journey with Live Activity on Lock Screen, get smart stop recommendations
- **Smart Filters** — Find pet areas, family restrooms, showers, RV parking, baby changing stations
- **Trusted Chains** — Prioritize favorites like Buc-ee's, Love's, Pilot, Wawa, and more
- **Widgets & Siri** — "Hey Siri, find nearby restrooms" plus home screen widgets
- **Community Ratings** — Real reviews from real travelers, contribute with a single tap

### Privacy First

RestMap processes everything on-device. No account required, no tracking, no data collection. Your location stays on your phone.

## Introduction

RestMap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
We access your device's location to show nearby restrooms. Location data is used in real-time and is **not stored on our servers**. Location is only accessed while you are actively using the app.

### User-Generated Content
Reviews and ratings you submit are stored **anonymously**. We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. Your contributions help other users find clean restrooms.

### Local Preferences
App settings (such as filter preferences and contribution statistics) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

- Display restrooms near your current location

- Allow you to contribute ratings and reviews
- Moderate content to ensure community safety
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage & Security

User-generated content (ratings and reviews) is stored securely using **Google Firebase** services.

- All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Reviews are stored anonymously with no connection to your identity
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not use your data for advertising

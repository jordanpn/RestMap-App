Frequently Asked Questions

How does RestMap know restroom quality?

RestRoom IQ combines 4 data sources: 600+ brand profiles, community ratings, OpenStreetMap (50,000+ US toilets), and Refuge Restrooms (accessibility data). The AI fuses these sources to predict quality with letter grades (A-F).

Is RestMap free to use?

Yes! RestMap is completely free to download and use. All core features including RestRoom IQ ratings, road trip planning, and community ratings are available at no cost.

Does RestMap show accessibility information?

Yes! RestMap shows wheelchair access, family restrooms, changing tables, and gender-neutral options. This data comes from OpenStreetMap and Refuge Restrooms, a database of safe, accessible restrooms.

What devices support RestMap?

RestMap is available for iPhone and iPad. It includes support for Live Activities on the Lock Screen, widgets, and Siri shortcuts for hands-free operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does RestMap know restroom quality?

RestMap uses AI-powered RestRoom IQ that analyzes data from 250+ chains and community ratings to predict quality with letter grades (A-F). The more the community contributes, the more accurate the predictions become.

Is RestMap free to use?

Yes! RestMap is completely free to download and use. All core features including RestRoom IQ ratings, road trip planning, and community ratings are available at no cost.

Does RestMap work offline?

RestMap requires an internet connection to search for locations and fetch ratings. However, your favorites and recent searches are cached for quick access, and the AI predictions work on-device for privacy.

What devices support RestMap?

RestMap is available for iPhone and requires iOS 17 or later. It includes support for Live Activities on the Lock Screen, widgets, and Siri shortcuts for hands-free operation.
